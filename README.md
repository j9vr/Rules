# Server Rules


[Public Link](<https://dxrp.net/embed/ruleset/019e3b90-3df0-7e8a-8a76-1269285a580a>)

Please read and follow these rules to ensure a positive experience for everyone.

---

## ⚠️ Important Notice

Violations of these rules may result in warnings, kicks, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.

Staff decisions are final. If you believe you were unfairly punished, you may appeal in our Discord.

---

# Roleplay Rules

### §1 — Stay In Character (IC)

- Remain in character during roleplay unless using approved out-of-character communication.
- Do not intentionally break immersion.

### §2 — No Random Deathmatch (RDM)

- Killing or attacking players without valid in-character reason is prohibited.
- Escalation and roleplay should happen before violence whenever possible.

### §3 — No Metagaming

- Using information learned outside of roleplay inside the game is prohibited.
- Examples include:
  - Using Discord voice chat for in-game advantages.
  - Acting on information from streams or clips.
  - Sharing hidden locations through OOC methods.

### §4 — No Powergaming

- Do not force actions on other players without giving them a chance to respond.
- Unrealistic actions or impossible abilities are not allowed.

### §5 — FearRP

- Value your character's life.
- Act realistically in dangerous situations.
- Running into gunfire or refusing reasonable demands without cause may be considered FailRP.

### §6 — No FailRP

- All roleplay should remain believable and realistic within the setting.
- Examples of FailRP:
  - Unrealistic behavior.
  - Ignoring severe injuries.
  - Randomly attacking everyone.
  - Jumping from tall buildings repeatedly without consequence.

### §7 — New Life Rule (NLR)

- After your character dies, you may not return to the area of death for a set amount of time.
- You cannot remember information directly related to your death.

### §8 — Combat Logging

- Disconnecting to avoid roleplay, death, arrest, or consequences is prohibited.

### §9 — Roleplay Before Gunplay

- Attempt roleplay interaction before resorting to violence whenever reasonable.
- Not every situation should immediately become a shootout.

### §10 — No Revenge Killing

- You may not repeatedly target players because of out-of-character anger.

### §11 — No Exploiting

- Exploiting bugs, glitches, map issues, or scripts is strictly forbidden.
- Report exploits to staff immediately.

### §12 — No Cheating

- Hacks, scripts, macros, third-party cheats, or unfair modifications are prohibited.
- Attempting to bypass bans or anti-cheat systems will result in permanent punishment.
- will also be a gobal ban from all servers

---

# Building Rules

### §13 — Respect Building Limits

- Do not create excessively large, laggy, or server-breaking builds.
- Staff may remove structures that negatively affect performance.

### §14 — No Prop Abuse

- Prop pushing, prop trapping, prop killing, or physics abuse is forbidden.
- Props may not be used to intentionally lag or grief players.

### §15 — Do Not Block Important Areas

- Do not block roads, spawn areas, shops, entrances, or roleplay locations.

### §16 — Cleanup Your Props

- Remove unused props and abandoned builds when leaving.

---

# Voice & Communication Rules

### §17 — Microphone Etiquette

- Excessive screaming, microphone spam, earrape, or soundboards are prohibited.
- Keep background noise to a minimum.

### §18 — No Voice Chat Harassment

- Do not insult, threaten, or target players over voice chat.

### §19 — Music & Soundboards

- Music and soundboards may only be used if they do not disrupt gameplay or annoy others.
- Staff may ask you to stop at any time.
